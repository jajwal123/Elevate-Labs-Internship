# Phishing Email Analysis Report

## Objective

Analyze a phishing email sample and identify phishing characteristics.

## Email Information

Sender:
support@paypal-security.com

Subject:
Urgent: Verify Your Account

## Phishing Indicators

### 1. Suspicious Sender

The sender email address is not an official PayPal domain.

### 2. Urgent Language

The email pressures the user to verify the account immediately.

### 3. Suspicious Link

The link points to a non-official PayPal website.

### 4. Generic Greeting

Uses "Dear Customer" instead of the user's name.

### 5. Social Engineering

Attempts to create fear by threatening account suspension.

## Conclusion

The email contains multiple phishing indicators and should not be trusted.
